About Me

Muhammad Naseem Ganji

Business Intelligence & ERP Specialist

Strategic Operations Leader

I’m a Toronto-based strategist with 15+ years of experience driving transformation across energy, finance, and public sector organizations. My work blends analytics, ERP systems, and stakeholder engagement to deliver measurable impact and service excellence.

I hold an MBA from Bakhat University, an Executive Masters in Economic Policy (EMEP) from the University of Central Asia, and a Diploma in Software & Web Development from Academy of Learning Career College. This unique blend of policy, business, and technical training allows me to bridge strategic vision with hands-on execution.

I’ve led national ERP rollouts, built KPI frameworks, and developed dashboards that inform executive decisions. My toolkit includes Power BI, SQL, Excel, Acumatica, and Dynamics 365 FO — all backed by PMP certification and a passion for modernization.

Whether I’m optimizing financial workflows, improving client experience, or guiding cross-sector teams, I bring clarity, precision, and purpose to every project. Let’s connect and explore how data and strategy can shape better outcomes.
